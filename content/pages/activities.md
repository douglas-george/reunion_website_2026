Nothing here is tied to a day or time — it's a pile of ideas to pull from. **Add whatever sounds fun.**

## On the beach
- Swimming and splashing
- Sandcastle building
- Sunset walk along the shore
- Beach games — volleyball, frisbee, paddleball
- A beach bonfire / s'mores (if the rental allows it)

## On the water
- Kayaks or paddleboards
- A boat or pontoon outing

## Around town
- Ice cream run
- Farmers market or a downtown stroll
- Mini golf

## Day trips
- The sand dunes
- A lighthouse
- A local state park

## Rainy day
- Board games and cards
- Movie afternoon
- Puzzles

*Toss in your own — this list is for everyone.*
