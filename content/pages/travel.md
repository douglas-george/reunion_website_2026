## The plan: caravan from Cleveland

We're all driving together — two cars leaving Cleveland on the morning of **Sunday, July 5**.
The full drive is about 4.5 hours, but we're breaking it up with a stop in Ann Arbor to visit
family along the way.

**Rough route:** Cleveland → I-80 W → I-94 W → Ann Arbor → I-94 W → Coloma, MI

## Ann Arbor stop

We'll swing through Ann Arbor for a visit with family mid-day on July 5. Exact timing TBD —
watch the group text for updates.

## Where we're going

**Monroe House**, Coloma, MI — just half a mile from Hagar Shore Beach and Roadside Park
Beach on Lake Michigan. The house is in a wooded area near the water, very easy to find once
you're in Coloma.

Plug **"Coloma, MI"** into your maps app and we'll share the exact address in the family
group text closer to the date.

## Arrival

We're aiming to arrive **Sunday afternoon**, July 5. Check-in details will be in the group
text. Once we're there, unload and claim your room — then beach!

## Heading home

We check out **Wednesday, July 8**. Plan to be packed and on the road by mid-morning for the
drive back to Cleveland.
