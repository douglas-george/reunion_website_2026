## On the water

Swimming off the main dock, kayaks and canoes in the boathouse (life jackets required),
and fishing off the quieter north dock. Bring a pole or borrow one of the lodge's.

## On land

- **Trails:** an easy 1-mile loop around the lake and a steeper 3-mile out-and-back to the
  overlook. Maps at the front desk.
- **Games:** cornhole, horseshoes, and a big field for whatever the kids invent.
- **The great room:** cards, board games, a piano nobody can really play.

## Rainy-day backups

If the weather turns, we move into the lodge: the slideshow, a euchre tournament, a movie
for the kids, and far too much time around the snack table.

## Off-site

A small town 15 minutes away has an ice-cream stand, a hardware store, and a surprisingly
good diner if anyone needs a morning out.
